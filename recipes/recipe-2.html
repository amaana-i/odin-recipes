Rice & Stew

Nicely cooked rice and stew ready for consumption !

Recipes

  • Rice
  • Salt
  • Tomato Paste
  • Onion
  • Mackerel
  • Meat

Steps

  1. Boil the rice, add Salt
  2. Steam the tomato paste together with the onion
  3. Pour water inside
  4. Cook it then add the Mackerel
  5. Add the meat
  6. Check the rice and stew, it's meal time !
