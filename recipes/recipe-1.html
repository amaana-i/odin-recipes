Banku

Finished Banku

This is how your final banku should look like when it is well-prepared

It is very delicious and mostly eaten by people in the coastal areas of Ghana !

Ingredients

  • Cassava Dough
  • Corn Dough
  • Okro
  • Pepper
  • Meat/Fish/Crabs

Steps

  1. Mix the cassava dough and corn dough to make it uniform
  2. Put on fire and stair/li>
  3. Stair till it becomes heavy and make it like TZ
  4. Your banku is ready
