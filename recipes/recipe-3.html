Goat Light Soup

Delicious Goat Light Soup !

Recipes

  • Goat meat
  • Salt
  • Tomato Paste
  • Onion
  • Pepper
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Steam the goat meat
  2. Grind the pepper, onion and garlic
  3. Pour it on the steammed meat
  4. Cook it on fire
  5. Your Delicious Goat Light Soup is ready
